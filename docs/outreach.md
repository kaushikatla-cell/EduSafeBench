# Outreach and Pilot Recruitment

## Goal

Recruit 5-15 early evaluators (teachers, CS club mentors, and advanced students) to validate benchmark usefulness and identify rubric gaps.

## Target profiles

- AP CSA/AP CSP teachers.
- High school CS club leads.
- Undergraduate peer tutors supporting intro programming.

## Outreach script

Subject: Request for 20-minute feedback on AI reliability benchmark for AP CS

Hello [Name],

I built EduSafeBench, an open benchmark that audits how reliable AI coding assistants are for AP CSA/AP CSP learners. I am recruiting a small pilot group to review results and share practical feedback.

What I am asking:
- 20 minutes to review one report.
- 2-3 comments on correctness, pedagogy, and classroom usability.
- Optional: whether this would affect tool choice or policy.

Repo: https://github.com/kaushikatla-cell/EduSafeBench

Thanks,
[Your Name]

## Evidence logging

Track each outreach attempt and response in a spreadsheet or markdown log with:

- Date contacted
- Role and institution
- Response status
- Feedback summary
- Actionable change made to benchmark
